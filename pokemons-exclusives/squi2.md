### ❓ Obtaining Method

To resurrect fossils, you must first build a **Resurrection Machine**.

This structure is made up of the following three blocks:

- `Fossil Analyzer`
- `Restoration Tank`
- `Data Monitor`

Once the machine has been assembled, you will be able to restore the fossils obtained throughout your adventure.

---

### 🔄 Usage

- 🖱️ Place a `Nether Star` and a `Mystic Water` inside the `Fossil Analyzer`.
- 🖱️ Connect a `Hopper` to the `Restoration Tank`, then add organic matter.
- 💤 Wait for the restoration process to finish, then catch the Pokémon with a Poké Ball.

### 📜 Description

> Created by Mewtwo from Ash's Squirtle, this second Squirtletwo took part in the battle between the cloned Pokémon and their original counterparts. The conflict came to an end when Ash was petrified while trying to stop the attacks of Mewtwo and Mew.
