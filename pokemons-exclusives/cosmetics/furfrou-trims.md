# 🐩 Furfrou - Trims

## 🐩 List of Furfrou Trims

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
Furfrou can change its trim style to adopt different appearances!
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## ✂️ How to Change Trims?

1. 🎨 Make Furfrou hold the dye corresponding to the desired trim.
   > Sneak, right-click on Furfrou, then select **"Change Cosmetic"**
2. ✂️ Use **Shears** on Furfrou.
3. ⚡ The dye is consumed and the new trim is applied.
