### ❓ Obtaining Method

To resurrect fossils, you must first build a **Resurrection Machine**.

This structure is made up of the following three blocks:

- `Fossil Analyzer`
- `Restoration Tank`
- `Data Monitor`

Once the machine has been assembled, you will be able to restore the fossils obtained throughout your adventure.

---

### 🔄 Usage

- 🖱️ Place a `Nether Star` and a `Miracle Seed` inside the `Fossil Analyzer`.
- 🖱️ Connect a `Hopper` to the `Restoration Tank`, then add organic matter.
- 💤 Wait for the restoration process to finish, then catch the Pokémon with a Poké Ball.
