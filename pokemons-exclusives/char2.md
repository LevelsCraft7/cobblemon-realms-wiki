### ❓ Obtaining Method

To resurrect fossils, you must first build a **Resurrection Machine**.

This structure is made up of the following three blocks:

- `Fossil Analyzer`
- `Restoration Tank`
- `Data Monitor`

Once the machine has been assembled, you will be able to restore the fossils obtained throughout your adventure.

---

### 🔄 Usage

- 🖱️ Place a `Nether Star` and a `Charcoal Stick` inside the `Fossil Analyzer`.
- 🖱️ Connect a `Hopper` to the `Restoration Tank`, then add organic matter.
- 💤 Wait for the restoration process to finish, then catch the Pokémon with a Poké Ball.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/Lost-Lore/char2_bulb_squi2/char2_bulb2_squir2.png)

### 📜 Description

> Charmandertwo was created by Dr. Fuji alongside the first Bulbasaurtwo and Squirtletwo in an attempt to bring his daughter Ambertwo back to life. However, it disappeared along with Ambertwo, Bulbasaurtwo, and Squirtletwo during the experiment.
