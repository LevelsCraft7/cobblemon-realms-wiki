# 🥋 Kubfu - Evolutions

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
These forms do not exist in the official Cobblemon mod. This is neither a bug nor an oversight. Although they may appear in the <code>Party</code> menu, they retain their normal appearance in-game. This is <strong>normal</strong>. Therefore, no images are displayed, but the alt text is preserved in case they are added officially in the future.

### 🎯 Form Ability

* **Ability:** Unseen Fist

---

### 📍 Item Locations

| 📜 Item | 🧭 Where to Find It |
|--------|---------------|
| **Scroll of Darkness** | End City, Dungeon, Village, Mineshaft, Nether Fortress, Desert Temple, Jungle Temple, Igloo, Mansion, Small & Large Underwater Ruins, Shipwreck Treasure, Pillager Outpost, Bastion Treasure, Ruined Portal, Ancient City, Ancient City Ice Box |
| **Archaeology** | Can also be obtained from **Suspicious Sand** using a **Brush** |

---

### 🔄 Usage

- 🖱️ Right-click Kubfu with the `Scroll of Darkness` item to assign this style.
- ℹ️ The choice is permanent for the evolution line.
> ![]()💡 The scroll is not consumed upon use.

---

### 📜 Description

> It lives in a distant mountainous region where it perfects its techniques and strengthens its legs by running along steep cliffs.
