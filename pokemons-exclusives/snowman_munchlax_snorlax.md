---

### 📜 Description

> Snowman Munchlax is a winter variant of Munchlax that has taken on the appearance of a snowman. Despite its peaceful look and warm smile, it still possesses its legendary appetite and spends most of its time searching for something to eat. It is said to appear during the coldest times of the year, bringing with it the spirit of winter and the holiday season.
