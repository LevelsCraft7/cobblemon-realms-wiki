# 💎 Crystal Onix & Crystal Steelix

Crystal Onix is a rare variant inspired by the Pokémon anime (Episode 87).  
It features a crystalline body and unique abilities not found in regular Onix.

---

## 📍 How to Obtain

- **Crystal Onix** spawns rarely in **caves and underground areas**.
- To obtain **Crystal Steelix**, evolve Crystal Onix normally:

➡️ **Trade + Metal Coat**

---

## 🪨 Crystal Onix

**Typing:** Steel  
**Stats:** Similar to regular Onix, but **+50 Base Stat Total**

**Abilities:**
- Water Absorb  
- Mirror Armor

---

## 🔩 Crystal Steelix

**Typing:** Steel  
**Stats:** Similar to regular Steelix, but **+50 Base Stat Total**

**Abilities:**
- Water Absorb  
- Crystalline  
  - Changes all **Rock-type moves into Steel-type moves**

### Mega Evolution

Crystal Steelix can Mega Evolve using:

- **Crystalline Steelixite**

---

## 💠 Crystal Mega Steelix

**Stats:**
- Similar to regular **Mega Steelix**
- **+50 Base Stat Total**
- **Attack and Special Attack stats are swapped**

**Ability:**
- Hall of Mirrors  
  - Using **Flash Cannon** lowers the target’s **Special Defense by 1 stage**

---

## 📌 Summary

- **Crystal Onix** is a rare cave spawn.  
- Evolves into **Crystal Steelix** using **Metal Coat + trade**.  
- Both forms gain **+50 BST** compared to their regular versions.  
- Crystal Steelix can **Mega Evolve** using **Crystalline Steelixite**.
