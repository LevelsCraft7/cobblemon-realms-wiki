![Crystal Onix](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/Mega-Shadown/onix_crystal.png) ![Crystal Onix Shiny](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/Mega-Shadown/onix_crystal_shiny.png)

---

### 🎯 Form Ability

- **Ability:** Fragile Crystal & Crystalline

---

### 🧬 Evolution

- Feed it 600 crystals (1 amethyst shard, 2 small buds, 4 medium buds, 6 large buds, and 8 clusters). After that, it can evolve at level 40+ at dawn.

---

### 📍 Location

| 🌍 Biome | 🌙 Condition |
|---|---|
| Overworld | Y ≤ 40 / Light Level ≤ 7 (Ultra Rare) |
| Dripstone Caves | Near diamond blocks (Ultra Rare) |

---

### 📜 Description

> As it digs through the ground, it absorbs solid materials, giving its body greater density.
