### 🔄 Usage

- 🖱️ Right-click Dialga while holding the `Time Gear`.
- ℹ️ No additional requirements are needed.
> 💡 The effect can be reversed by unequipping the item.

---

### 📜 Description

> Primal Dialga is an altered form of Dialga that appeared following the paralysis of Temporal Tower. Deprived of its balance, the Temporal Pokémon descended into madness and became extremely aggressive, condemning the future to a world frozen in time.
