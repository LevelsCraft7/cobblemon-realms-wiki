# 🌍 Regional Biases

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
This category contains Pokémon with a predisposition to evolve into a specific regional form. These variants make it possible to obtain certain regional evolutions even when no official regional pre-evolution exists.
</p>
{% endhint %}

These Pokémon:

- 🌍 Are linked to a specific regional form
- 🔄 Can evolve into their associated regional form
- ⚙️ May sometimes evolve into their standard form under certain conditions

---

## 📋 Available Regional Biases

Below is the list of currently available regional biases.

> *This list may be expanded in future updates.*
