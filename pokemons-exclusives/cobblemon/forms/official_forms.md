# ✨ Official Forms

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
This category contains appearances inspired by official Pokémon media. Although these appearances have been featured in official Pokémon works, they are generally not available in modern mainline games.
</p>
{% endhint %}

They can originate from various sources:

- 📺 The Pokémon anime
- 🎮 Video games
- 📖 Manga
- 🎴 Trading cards
- 📚 Other official media

---

## 📋 Available Forms

Below is the list of currently available official forms.
