# 🎭 Miscellaneous Forms

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
This category contains various special appearances that do not fit into any of the other available form categories. They come from a wide range of inspirations, references to the Pokémon anime, or exclusive variants created specifically for Cobblemon.
</p>
{% endhint %}

These appearances have several distinctive characteristics:

- 🎨 Each form has its own unique origin or inspiration
- 🌍 Acquisition methods vary depending on the appearance
- 📺 Some are inspired by the Pokémon anime
- 🧩 They do not belong to any other form category
- ✨ Several feature an exclusive shiny appearance

---
