# ⛏️ Minecraft Forms

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
This category contains appearances inspired by the world of Minecraft. These variants usually incorporate iconic elements from the game into their design and are exclusive to Cobblemon.
</p>
{% endhint %}

These appearances can be inspired by various aspects of Minecraft:

- ⛏️ Blocks and materials
- 🌍 Biomes
- 👾 Iconic creatures
- 🏗️ Structures and environments
- ✨ Signature elements from the Minecraft universe

---

## 📋 Available Forms

Below is the list of currently available Minecraft forms.

> *This list may be expanded in future updates.*
