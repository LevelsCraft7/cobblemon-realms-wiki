# 🎨 Special Forms

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
This page presents the different categories of special forms available.
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
<strong>Special forms</strong> include all <strong>visual variants</strong> available in the modpack. Unlike official Pokémon forms, these variants are purely cosmetic and do <strong>not</strong> affect the stats or abilities of the Pokémon they apply to.
</p>

---

## 📚 Available Categories

---

### ✨ Official Forms

This category contains forms originating from the official Pokémon universe. Whether they come from the anime, manga, or older video games, these appearances have existed in Pokémon media, even if they are no longer present in modern mainline games.

---

### ⛏️ Minecraft Forms

These appearances are inspired by the world of Minecraft. Blocks, iconic creatures, and references to the game can all be found in the design of these exclusive forms.

---

### 🌍 Regional Biases

Some Pokémon have a natural predisposition to evolve into a specific regional form. This category gathers the pre-evolutions associated with these regional variants and explains how they work.

---

### 🎣 Pokémon: Magikarp Jump Forms

Inspired by the mobile game **Pokémon: Magikarp Jump**, these appearances recreate the many special Magikarp patterns introduced in the game. Each pattern has its own style and can be obtained in different ways.

---

### 🐉 Gyarados Jump Patterns

Magikarp from **Pokémon: Magikarp Jump** retain their unique appearance after evolving. This category showcases the various Gyarados variants derived from them, added specifically by Cobblemon.

---

### 🎭 Miscellaneous Forms

Some appearances do not belong to any specific category. This section gathers all unique or unusual variants that do not fit elsewhere.

---

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
Some appearances require the corresponding built-in resource pack to be enabled in order to be visible.
</p>
{% endhint %}
