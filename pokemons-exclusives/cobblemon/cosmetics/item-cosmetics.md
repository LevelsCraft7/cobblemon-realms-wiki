# 🕶️ Cosmetic Article

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
These cosmetic modifications consist of equipping an accessory in a Pokémon’s cosmetic slot. They are applied uniformly across all of its variants, including Shiny forms, regional forms, and gender-based differences.
</p>
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
Some crafting recipes are currently not working; they have already been reported
</p>
{% endhint %}
