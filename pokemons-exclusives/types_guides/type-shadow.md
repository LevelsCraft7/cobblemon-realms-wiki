# 📖 Shadow Type Overview

The **Shadow type** has its own mechanics:

- ✨ Terastallization (Tera)
- 💠 Exclusive Z-Crystal
- ⚔️ Dedicated moves
- 📊 Unique type chart
- 🌈 Signature Dynamax Move

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
At the moment, the only <strong>Shadow-type</strong> Pokémon are <strong>Shadow Mewtwo</strong> and <strong>Shadow Lugia</strong>.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

# 🌑 Shadow Energy

To transform a Pokémon into its **Shadow form**, a **Shadow Energy** is required.

- ❤️ The Pokémon must have at least **250 friendship**.
- 🔄 Using the item resets its friendship to **0**.

# 🌑 Shadow-type Moves

## 🕳️ Black Vortex
> 💠 **Signature Shadow-type Z-Move**

- Requires **Shadowium-Z**.
- Damage is calculated based on the move used as the base.

---

## 🌈 Max Void
> ⭐ **Signature Shadow-type Dynamax Move**

- ⚡ Raises the **Speed** of the user and its allies.
- 💥 Damage is calculated based on the move used as the base.
