# 🪶 Silver Wing

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Silver Wing</strong> is a held item that enhances <strong>Lugia</strong>'s abilities.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## ⚙️ Effect

When held by **Lugia**, the Silver Wing grants:

- ✨ +50% Special Attack
- 🌙 +50% Special Defense

---

## 🧭 Acquisition

The Silver Wing can be obtained in two ways:

- 🏛️ Capture **Lugia Temple** (it already holds the item)
- 💼 Occasional purchase from a **Master-level Entrepreneur Villager**

---

## 📌 Note

- Item exclusively linked to Lugia
- Can be obtained directly through capture or rare trading
