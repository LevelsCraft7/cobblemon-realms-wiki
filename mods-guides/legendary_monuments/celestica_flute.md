# 🎶 Celestica Flute

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Celestica Flute</strong> is a progression item used to craft the <strong>Azure Flute</strong>.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 🧭 Acquisition

The Celestica Flute can be obtained in two ways:

- 💼 Trade with a **Master-level Entrepreneur Villager**
- 📦 Reward chests in the **Turnback Cave**

---

## 🛠️ Usage

This item is used as a component in the crafting of the **Azure Flute**.

---

## ⚠️ Note

- Non-consumable item
- No use in combat or exploration
- A progression element toward Arceus
