# 🌌 Cosmic Catalyst

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Cosmic Catalyst</strong> is an item used to collect <strong>Cosmic Dust</strong> and track progression of certain collection systems.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 🧭 Main Function

The Cosmic Catalyst has two main uses:

- ✨ Collect **Cosmic Dust** by right-clicking
- 📊 View collection progression (Cosmic Dust and Footprints)

---

## 🧪 Acquisition

Once in possession of the **Cosmic Catalyst**, you can:

- 🪐 Gather Cosmic Dust
- 🌟 Progress linked to [Cosmog](https://our-story-network.gitbook.io/cr-wiki/legendary-monuments/legendary_monuments/gen_7#cosmog)

---

## ⚠️ Secondary Use

When right-clicking something other than **Cosmic Dust**, the catalyst allows you to view:

- 📊 Cosmic Dust progression
- 👣 Footprint progression
