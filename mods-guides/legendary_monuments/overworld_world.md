- 📌 Biome: Dyna Plains

## 📌 Features

- 🌳 Structure centered around a giant tree
- 📦 Hidden chest at the base
- 🍎 Unique loot related to Galar systems

---

## 🛠️ Usage

Dyna Fruits are mainly used for:

- ⚱️ Crafting special urns
- 🕊️ Progression towards Galar Legendary events
