# 🛡️ Regi Pauldron

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Regi Pauldron</strong> are equippable items placed in the shoulder slot, granting various stat bonuses and effects.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## ⚙️ Main Function

The Regi Pauldron are equipped in the dedicated slot and apply passive effects to the player.

---

## 🧭 List of Pauldron

There are 6 different Pauldron:

- 🐉 **Dragon Pauldron** → +3 damage
- ⚡ **Electric Pauldron** → +20% speed
- 🪨 **Rock Pauldron** → Haste effect
- ❄️ **Ice Pauldron* → Luck effect
- 🛡️ **Steel Pauldron* → +3 armor
- 👑 **Titan Pauldron** → combines all effects

---

## 🛠️ Crafting

### 🔧 Standard Pauldron

All 5 base Pauldron share a similar recipe:

- Each variant uses its **specific ore**
- Result: a type-related **Pauldron**

---

### 👑 Titan Pauldron

The **Titan Pauldron** is crafted by combining:

- 🧱 The 5 base Pauldron
- 💠 1 **Titan Core**

---

## ⚠️ Important Note

- 💠 The **Titan Core** is obtained when a Regigigas statue appears
- ⚖️ It is only dropped **once per appearance**
- 👉 You must choose between:
  - 🛡️ Titan Core
  - 🔨 Titan Hammer
