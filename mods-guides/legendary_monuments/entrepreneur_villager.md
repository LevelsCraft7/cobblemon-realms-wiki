# 💼 Entrepreneur Villager

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Entrepreneur Villager</strong> is a special villager that uses the <strong>Suitcase Block</strong> as a job site block and offers various items related to progression systems.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 🧭 Main Function

The Entrepreneur Villager offers rare and utility items related to mod progression.

It can be found:

- 🧳 In villages at its Suitcase Block
- 🏕️ At the **Outskirt Stand,**

---

## 💼 Job Site Block

The block used by the Entrepreneur is:

- 🧳 **Suitcase Block**

---

## 🛒 Trading

It offers various items, including:

- 🍬 **Poketreat Box**
- 📦 Various progression and crafting items
