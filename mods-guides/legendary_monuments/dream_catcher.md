# 🪶 Dream Catcher

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Dreamcatcher</strong> is an item placed near a bed that grants the <strong>Lucid Dream</strong> effect during sleep.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 🧭 Main Function

The Dream Catcher must be placed on a roof, near a bed.

When a player sleeps nearby, they receive the following effect:

- 🌙 **Lucid Dream**

---

## ✨ Lucid Dreaming Effect

While the effect is active, each night of sleep may grant:

- 🌑 10% chance to obtain a **Nightmare Essence**
- 🌕 10% chance to obtain a **Lunar Feather**

⚠️ These two items cannot be obtained during the same night.

---

## 🧪 Acquisition

The Dream Catcher can be obtained in two ways:

- 🧵 Crafted with **9 Dream Threads**
- 💼 Purchased from the **Entrepreneur Villager**

![Dream Catcher crafting recipe](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/Craft-recettes/craft_legendary_monuments/dream_catcher.png)

---

## 🛠️ Placement

- 📍 Must be placed on a roof
- 🛏️ Must be near a bed
- 🌙 Only works while the player is sleeping
