# 🏛️ Pedestal System

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
<strong>Pedestals</strong> are the core of the <strong>Legendary Monuments</strong> system, allowing the summoning of most Legendary and Mythical Pokémon.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## ⚙️ Main Function

Pedestals are used to summon Legendary and Mythical Pokémon.

👉 Simply place the required item on the pedestal to trigger the corresponding Pokémon’s summoning.

---

## 🧭 Usage

- 📦 Place the required item on the pedestal
- ✨ Automatically triggers the Pokémon’s appearance
- ⚔️ The summoned Pokémon appears immediately nearby

---

## 🎨 Features

- 🏛️ Each pedestal has a unique model and theme
- 🎭 Textures vary depending on the associated Pokémon

---

## 👥 Player-Based System

Pedestals work **individually per player**:

- ✔ Each player can summon their own Pokémon
- ✔ Multiple players can use the same pedestal
- ✔ No blocking between players on servers

---

## 📌 Note

The Pedestal system is used for the majority of Legendary and Mythical Pokémon in the mod.
