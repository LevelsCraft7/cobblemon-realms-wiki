## ⛏️ Mechanics

It is not necessary to dig the entire surrounding area.

---

## 🎲 Weight System

Each item has a **weight** that determines its spawn probability.

- The higher the weight, the more common the item is  
- Example: an item with a weight of 15 is **3× more likely** than an item with a weight of 5  
- Exact probability = item weight ÷ total weight sum of the table

---

## 🍀 Luck System

The **Luck** attribute influences loot probabilities:

- ➕ Positive luck increases all weights  
- ➖ Negative luck reduces weights (minimum -10)  
- High luck can make rare items more accessible  
- Negative luck can remove some very rare items from the pool

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
💡 In standard survival gameplay, Minecraft does not allow luck bonuses.
<br>
CobbleSafari adds this system via the <strong>Lucky Miner Helmet</strong>.
</p>
{% endhint %}
