# 🤝 Trade List

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
During your adventures, you may encounter various CobbleSafari Merchants.
Unlike regular villagers, they do not use Emeralds, but instead have their own currencies and trade items.
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
Merchants offer exclusive trades related to the various CobbleSafari activities. Each merchant has several variants, each with its own item pool and a random number of available trades.
</p>

---

## ⛏️ Hiker

**Hikers** can be encountered in the Sinnoh Underground.
They are mainly interested in the various spheres obtained during your excavations.

There are three variants:

- 🟡 Small Sphere Hiker
- 🔴 Large Sphere Hiker
- 💎 Treasure Hiker

---

# 🟡 Small Sphere Hiker

The **Small Sphere Hiker** mainly trades small spheres for common items and various useful resources.

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
This variant offers between 5 and 8 different trades.
</p>
{% endhint %}
