# ⛏️ Sinnoh Underground

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
After passing through a strange portal, you find yourself in the <strong>Sinnoh Underground</strong>! Beneath the surface lies a vast network of forgotten tunnels containing treasures, fossils, mysterious spheres, and abandoned ancient secret bases. Between excavation mini-games, Pokémon Hideaways, and encounters with other explorers, every expedition brings its share of surprises.
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
<em>Some extremely rare fossils can even guarantee obtaining a <strong>Shiny Pokémon</strong>.</em>
</p>

---

## 🚪 How to Access It?

The Sinnoh Underground can only be accessed through a **[Hoopa Portal](hoopa_portals.md)**.

If you are lucky enough, the portal's destination may be the Underground.
<br>
The confirmation screen always displays the destination before teleportation.

Once inside:

- ❌ Breaking blocks is impossible;
- ❌ Placing blocks is impossible;
- ⏳ Time is limited to **15 minutes** by default.

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
The Underground is a temporary dimension.
Once the portal disappears, all players still inside are automatically expelled.
</p>
{% endhint %}

# ⛏️ Excavation Mini-Game

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
While exploring the tunnels, you may occasionally notice unusual reflections on certain walls.
<br>
These special locations, called <strong>excavation sites</strong>, appear within tunnel walls.
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
ℹ️ Simply right-clicking the wall starts the excavation.
</p>

![Excavation Mini-Game](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/CobbleSafari/excavation.png)

---

## 🎮 How to Play?

The mechanics are directly inspired by the Underground mini-game from Pokémon Diamond & Pearl.
<br>
🎯 Your objective is simple: uncover the hidden treasures before the wall collapses.

To do so, you have two tools:

- ⛏️ **The Pickaxe**, precise but slow;
- 🔨 **The Hammer**, more powerful but much riskier.

Each strike removes part of the dirt and gradually reveals:

- 💎 treasures;
- 🪨 iron deposits;
- 🟫 different layers of rock.

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
An item is only obtained if its entire pattern is visible.
Even a single covered piece will cause it to be lost.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## ⚠️ Wall Stability

Each strike gradually reduces the wall's stability.

The cracks visible at the top of the screen allow you to estimate its condition:

- the farther they progress to the left;
- the closer the collapse becomes.

🔨 The Hammer:

- consumes more stability;
- applies a penalty if used too early.

Iron deposits are also dangerous and cause additional stability loss.

{% hint style="danger" %}
<p align="center">
When stability reaches zero, the wall collapses.
The mini-game ends immediately.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 💎 Treasures

Each treasure has:

- a unique shape;
- a different size;
- a more or less significant weight.

Some items are very common, while others are extremely rare.
<br>
The complete list of available rewards can be found on the following page:

👉 [💎 Sinnoh Underground Treasures](underground_treasure_list.md)

# 🥾 Hikers

Even underground, you are never completely alone...

**Hikers** also roam the tunnels in search of riches.

Most of them offer trades for your spheres, while some directly buy the treasures discovered during your excavations.

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
Never underestimate spheres.
They are worth much more than they seem.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

# 🌿 Pokémon Hideaways

**Pokémon Hideaways** are special areas that use the spawners from **Cobblemon Trial Edition**.
<br>
These isolated caverns are home to various Pokémon that you can battle or capture.
<br>
Defeating all Pokémon present grants various rewards.

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
Without Cobblemon Trial Edition, no Pokémon will appear.
However, the cave will remain fully explorable.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

# 🏠 Abandoned Secret Bases

Some old bases seem to have been deserted for nearly twenty years.

If you notice a strange opening in a wall, take the time to investigate: an old secret base may be hidden behind it. You will then be able to recover the **flag** left on the PC and use it in your own **[Secret Base PC](secret_base_pc.md)**.
