# 🌌 Distortion World

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
After passing through a strange portal, you find yourself in the <strong>Distortion World</strong>!
In this dimension where the laws of space and gravity no longer truly make sense, you will have to progress through floating platforms, illusion labyrinths, and ancient forgotten ruins.
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
<em>You may even discover the mysterious <strong>Distorted Pokémon</strong> and Giratina's secrets...</em>
</p>

---

## 🚪 How to Access It?

The Distortion World can only be accessed through a **Hoopa Portal**.

When a portal appears, its destination is displayed on the confirmation screen before teleportation. With a bit of luck, it may lead to the Distortion World.

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
Not all Hoopa Portals lead to the Distortion World.
<br>
Always remember to check the displayed destination before entering.
</p>
{% endhint %}

Once inside, several restrictions apply:

- ❌ Breaking blocks is impossible;
- ❌ Placing blocks is impossible;
- ⏳ Time inside is limited to **15 minutes** by default.

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
When the portal disappears, the dimension collapses and all players still inside are automatically expelled.
</p>
{% endhint %}

Like all special dimensions, the behavior of the Distortion World can be fully customized using the following configurations:

- [Dimensional Restrictions](https://cobblesafari.maxigregrze.fr/en/addons/config_dimensional_restrictions_config)
- [Dimensional Timer](https://cobblesafari.maxigregrze.fr/en/addons/config_dimensional_timer_config)

## 🪨 Platforms and Gravity

In the Distortion World, the concepts of up and down no longer truly make sense. Some islands float in the void, the floor can become the ceiling, and even liquids seem to defy the laws of gravity. To progress, you may need to chain together a few carefully placed jumps on rocks or flowers to reach the most distant areas.

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
Falling into the void immediately expels you from the Distortion World and sends you back to your entry point. You will then be unable to return until a new portal appears.
</p>
{% endhint %}

*Fortunately, Hoopa's magic prevents you from drifting endlessly through this chaotic space.*

![Distorted Platform](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/CobbleSafari/distortion_world/platforming.png)

## 🌿 Illusion Labyrinths

Not everything you see is necessarily real.

Some plants appear dangerous, covered in thorns capable of harming the careless... until you get close enough to discover that they are only an illusion.

Others are much more deceptive:

- some disappear;
- others suddenly appear;
- some can turn a simple path into a true labyrinth.

{% hint style="danger" %}
<p align="center">
Getting lost in an illusion labyrinth is easier than it seems.
</p>
{% endhint %}

![Distorted Labyrinth](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/CobbleSafari/distortion_world/labyrinth.png)

## ⚔️ Arena and Distorted Pokémon

Deep within the dimension lie the ruins of an ancient temple.

Fragments of the **Red Chain**, once created by the Lake Guardians, have left behind a strange energy taking the form of gigantic shadows: the **Distorted Pokémon**.

These creatures:

- are much stronger than their normal counterparts;
- cannot be captured;
- may drop **Red Chain Fragments** after being defeated.

{% hint style="success" %}
<p align="center">
Although these creatures resemble Pokémon, they are actually manifestations of the Distortion World's energy.
</p>
{% endhint %}

![Distorted Arena](https://cobblesafari.maxigregrze.fr/assets/img_distortion_arena-Yl0n4JFb.png)

## 🌀 Turnback Cave and Giratina

The only exit from the Distortion World leads to **Turnback Cave**.
This is where Giratina resides, silently observing the visitors who venture into its domain.

You are probably not the one it is waiting for...

However, Giratina accepts **Red Chain Fragments** and can transform them into mysterious **Distorted Red Crystals**.

![Turnback Cave and Giratina](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/CobbleSafari/distortion_world/turnback_cave_giratina.png)
