# 🧪 Secret Base PC

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
The <strong>Secret Base PC</strong> is a craftable block that allows you to create and manage your own secret base!
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
Unlike the Secret Bases from the Pokémon DPPt games, this PC is not used to break rocks.
</p>

<p align="center">
Instead, it functions more like a <strong>Beacon</strong>, but with mechanics entirely redesigned around Cobblemon: the <strong>flags found in the Underground Bases</strong> allow you to unlock powerful passive effects for your area.
</p>

---

## ⚙️ How to Use It?

- 🖱️ **Right-click** the block to open the PC interface
- 🏁 Insert your **collected flags** into the central slot and click **"Capture"**
- 🔋 Each flag fills the **PC battery**
- ❌ A flag cannot be inserted if the battery is full (except for special upgrade cases)
- 🔄 The arrows allow you to **switch the active effect**
- ⚡ Click **"Activate"** to enable or disable the effect

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
Breaking the PC allows it to be recovered as an item.  
The <strong>level, battery, and progression are preserved</strong> within the item.
</p>
{% endhint %}

## 🏅 PC Ranks

The PC has several **evolution ranks**, determined by the flags used.

Each rank corresponds to a color:

- 🔵 **Rank 0**: Blue (starting rank)
- 🟤 **Copper Rank**
- ⚪ **Silver Rank**
- 🟡 **Gold Rank**
- 🟣 **Platinum Rank**

---

### 📈 What a Rank Changes

Each rank upgrade improves:

- 🔋 the **maximum battery capacity**
- 📏 the **effect range**
- ⚙️ the **number of available effects**
- 💰 the **effect activation cost**

![PC Interface](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/CobbleSafari/pc_interface.png)

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
To upgrade your PC, simply insert a flag of the corresponding rank.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 🌟 Available Effects

The Secret Base PC offers 5 types of effects:

### 🚫 Pokémon Repel
Prevents Pokémon from spawning within the area.

{% hint style="success" %}
<p align="center">
This effect consumes <strong>no battery</strong>, regardless of rank.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

### 🍀 Spawn Boosts

- 🟢 **Uncommon** Spawn
- 🔵 **Rare** Spawn
- 🟣 **Ultra-Rare** Spawn
- ✨ **Shiny** Spawn

These effects:

- increase the spawn chances of targeted Pokémon;
- consume battery while active;
- are gradually unlocked based on the PC rank.

---

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
PC effects are based on the mechanics of <strong><a href="perfumes_incense_repels.md">Scents and Incenses</a></strong>.
<br>
Their strength is therefore directly tied to these systems.
</p>
{% endhint %}

---

## 🧪 Creative Mode & Administration

A special **creative flag** exists.

When inserted into a PC:

- 🔋 infinite battery
- ⚙️ all costs are removed
- 🌍 unlimited effects

{% hint style="danger" %}
<p align="center">
Warning: the PC remains breakable and editable even in Creative Mode.  
It is strongly recommended to <strong>secure it inside a protected structure</strong>.
</p>
{% endhint %}
