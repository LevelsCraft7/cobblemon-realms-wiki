![Wo-Chien](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/Myth_Legends/gen_9/wochien.png) ![Wo-Chien Shiny](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/LevelsCraft7/cobblemon-realms-wiki/refs/heads/main/imgwiki/Myth_Legends/gen_9/wochien_shiny.png)

---

### 🎯 Form Ability

- **Ability :** Tablets of Ruin

---

### 🗝️ How to Obtain

To obtain Wo-Chien, you must collect the `8 Grasswither Stakes` by right-clicking them. They can be found in any Nether biome.

Once you have obtained the `8 Grasswither Stakes`, you can interact with the `Grasswither Shrine` in the Nether to make **Level 50 Wo-Chien** appear, with a **2% chance of being Shiny**.

---

### 📜 Description

> It absorbs energy from surrounding vegetation, instantly drying out nearby forests and rendering fields barren.
