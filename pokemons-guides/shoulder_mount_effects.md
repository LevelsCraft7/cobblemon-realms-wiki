# 🎒 Shoulder Pokémon Effects (v5.0)

## 🎒 Shoulder Pokémon Effects (v5.0)

{% hint style="warning" %}
<p align="center">
The information on this page is no longer up to date. Since the related files/folders are no longer present in the datapack, these features are currently <strong>on hold</strong> until they are reintroduced into the modpack.
</p>
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
<p align="center">
<strong>Shoulder mounts</strong> are Pokémon that can be placed on your shoulders to grant you <strong>unique passive bonuses</strong>. Use the <code>/shoulder</code> command to view the full list of available shoulder mounts and their effects. To equip a shoulder mount, release your Pokémon, hold <code>Shift + Right Click</code> on it, then select <strong>Shoulder</strong>. Some effects require equipping two shoulder mounts simultaneously to activate.
</p>
{% endhint %}

<p align="center">
ℹ️ <em>Effects remain active as long as the Pokémon stays on your shoulder.</em>
</p>

## ⚙️ Information

- Multiple effects can be active at the same time.
- The amplifier corresponds to the effect level:
  - Level I = `0`
  - Level II = `1`
  - Level III = `2`
- Some effects have been adjusted for modpack balancing purposes.
